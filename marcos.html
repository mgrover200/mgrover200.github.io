MG-ROVER INFORMATION PAGES

Special request from family member, featuring ownership of their classic vehicle during the 1970's. The kit car built fibreglass body attached to box frame chassis 3M/5849 and powered by a Ford Zodiac V6 engine, was first registered XNR 778J on March 1st 1971. With  parts and fittings sourced from numerous vehicles, most probably included from Triumph, Vauxhall, MG, Austin, Porsche, Commer, and Hillman Hunter etc. 

WETHERBY NORTH YORKSHIRE

Here we have a couple of randomly selected photo's, probably from well over 40 years ago. This venue was at the Turnpike Motel in 1976 just off the A1 on a really cold february morning. Preserved from this meeting are a good number of photos, including cine film footage.

SKIPTON NORTH YORKSHIRE

This local meeting, later in the year, was at the Cross Keys during May 1976. Regarded as the year of the heatwave and subsequent drought. But was wet and windy, during this earlier photo session.

MARCOS RALLY JUNE 1977

Shown here at Castle Donington leicestershire, after a drive down the M1 in convoy. When meeting up with several other marcos owners at the Wooley Edge services, just south of Wakefield during June 1977.

MARCOS OWNERS AND ADMIRERS 

OF THIS CLASSIC SPORTS CAR 

Marcos cars were always admired in the early years, from well known names to those who for a period of time actually owned one, listed here are a just a random few from our own list which has been compiled over the years. Rod Stewart (Singer) before he upgraded to Lamborghinis. Paul Stassino (Actor) Alan Ball (Chelsea FC) Mick Robertson (Magpie TV Presenter) John Noakes (Blue Peter) Andy Fairweather Low lead singer of Amen Corner, who had one commisioned in Pink. Simon Park (of 'eye level' fame, the Van der Valk TV Series theme tune 1972). Scott Engel and John Maus of The Walker Brothers fame both owned one each in red and blue.


An old edition of..Record Mirror in our collection from February 1966...shows them looking out of the sunroof while kneeling on the transmission tunnel in each of their cars. Others not necessarily owners, but possibly used the vehicles for films, racing, even photo shoots etc, were Jackie Stewart, John Lennon, Cliff Richard and including the Beatles. Roger Moore (Saint TV Series) As a racing driver, drove a white Marcos in an early February 1969 episode. 'The World Beater' this along with many more in our list of well known owners and admirers, of this classic car. 

Weston Park June 1989
