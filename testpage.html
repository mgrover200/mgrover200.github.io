To release the fuel filter unions a 14mm and 19mm open ended spanners were both used as shown. Then an 8mm spanner for releasing the fuel filter from the bulkhead holding clamp. More Info.

Fitting the oil filter, without it first being 'primed' is bad news. the engine will be starved of lubrication during the critical few seconds, it will take before it is actually filled, and ready to begin circulating. More Info.
