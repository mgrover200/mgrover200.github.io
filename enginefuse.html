MG-ROVER INFORMATION PAGES

Information page concentrates specifically on our 2004 MG ZR engine bay fuse box, and which circuit each fuse, fusible links, and relays serves and protects. Contents will vary between different models and years, and so should only be regarded, as a basic reference guide.

F1 Engine management system, automatic transmission selector, speedo 10amp (red)
F2 Engine management system, fuel pump 20amp (yellow)
F3 Engine management glow plug relay 10amp (red)
F4 Aircon relay, cooling fan relay 10amp (red).

F5 Anti theft alarm, and CDL 25amp (clear) F6 Front fog lamps 15amp (blue) F7 Hazard lights, indicators (15amp (blue) F8 Engine management 15amp (blue) F9 Air conditioning 10amp (red) F10 Fuel pump 20amp (yellow).

The different types of fuses, and relays shown here as an example, may vary due to production year and model, and may contain more or less than the items which are actually included in the fuse box.

FL 1 Alternator 150 mega amp. FL 2 Aux ignition 40 amp. FL 3 Ignition 40 amp. FL 4 Cabin fuse box 40 amp. FL 5 Not required. FL 6 Glow plug 80 amp. Mega fusible links are the metal type, the highest rated one is 150 mega amp for the alternator..

Midi Fusible Links: FL 7 Starter 40 amp (green) FL 8 Cooling 20 amp (blue) FL 9 Lights 40 amp (green) FL 10 Blower 30 amp (pink) FL 11 Cooling 30 amp (pink) FL 12 ABS 40 amp (green). Relay 1 Fuel Pump. Relay 2 Starter Motor.Relay 3 Cooling Fan. Relay 4 Main Relay. Relay 5 Blower Motor. Relay 6 Front Fog Lights. Relay 7 A/C Compressor.

All information on this site, applies 'only' to our own 1996 Rover 200 Series and the 2004 MG-ZR  .
