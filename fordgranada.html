MG-Rover Technical and Information Pages

General maintenance, along with various other information of the Rover 200 and MGZR

On this special features page are listed a collection of family owned vehicles, over a period of time, a very long period of time actually. These were to include. Triumph. Vauxhall. Marcos. and Ford. With the most popular being the Vauxhall, consisting of Viva. Magnum. Victor and the VX2300. and for a short time the 1975 Rover P6 3500 V8. On this page, we are looking at the Ford Granada 2.8 GL from the early 1980's  

1981 Ford Granada 2.8 GL Automatic 

In crystal green with grey Interior, first registered in August 1981.  went on to give over 23 years of trouble free motoring, both here and abroad, and especially around North Wales. The smooth ride of the granada, enabled us to produce a numerous collection of  'Armchair Motoring Videos' over many years, from the late 1980's onwards.

Into the 90's shell suits and CB Radios.

From information supplied to enable this feature. It appears that although the above photo. was taken when the car was only around 3 years old, it looked dated and basic. As shown here this was to change in the late 80's. With the fitting of a body styling kit, new ghia styled grill, and modification to the door mirrors. Other slight changes to the interior, would include adapting all 4 manual windows to electric etc.
