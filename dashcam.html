DVR 207 DASHCAM 2013 to 2014  

Opposed to using the supplied screen mount, an alloy support bracket was created and is shown located above right of rear view mirror, with the viewing screen in a closed position

2014 to present and now with front and rear remote activated mobius cameras being installed, much smaller than the above DVR 207 about the size of a 'tic tac' box, and compact, no viewing screen makes for a secure and stealth fitting on this particular model of 'clown catchers' for setting up the parameters we use the Mobius mSetup Version 3.0.1.0   

REAR FITTED CAMERA  

Centre position of the rear located mobius camera, shown slipped into the supplied holding bracket, discreet under the spoiler and with the power cable fed along inside of the rear trim panel.    

To power up the mobius monitoring system a drivers side dashboard located button switch activates each camera simultaneuosly, power is supplied using dual USB cables. the 16gb micro sd cards we have installed in the cameras, have a combined eight hours of recording time before being over written, which are sufficient to review any incidents.
