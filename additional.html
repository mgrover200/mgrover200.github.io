ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FEATURES FOR THE 200/25/ZR    

French German Spanish Dutch Italian Russian

COLD WEATHER TYRE PRESSURE CHECK  

A change in temperature will affect your tyre pressure in cold weather. and will lower the psi on each of the vehicles tyres. I have my own tyre pressures set at 28psi all round, so if they all show an equal drop of say 4psi then no further air needs to be added....its temporary at this time.  

SMALL DIAMETER CABLE OIL INJECTOR  

Cycle cable oil injector not sure of its origins could be manufactured or home made, part filled with light oil, then used to lubricate or ease off small diameter cables, bonnet release cable,accelerator cable etc, including most vehicles with a manual operated choke..  

DISC HOLDER FOR SINGLE PLAY HEAD UNITS  

The passenger sun visor is used to retain an additional cd disc on a vehicle with a single play head unit installed, the disc shown is  just clipped onto cd inlay case centre grip, after its been cut to a smaller diameter and bonded onto the visor....saves looking around for it.

ROVER 200 SERIES ALTERNATOR SHIELD  

Alloy semi contoured, and quick release alternator weather shield, gives some protection during any extreme driving, especially bad conditions that are encountered along rain lashed motorways.  

TOOL TRAY AND BATTERY SAFETY CHECK   

Makes an ideal area for small hand tools, and to place spark plugs,
coil packs, and other small items on, with no chance of spanners shorting the electrics, one piece natural vented design which  just slips over battery, also protects terminals from oxidation.

REMOTE BATTERY TRICKLE FEED OPTION   

This direct power trickle charge 'click and connect' option, running directly from our 12volt battery charger, saved a lot of hassle of the
bonnet being opened especially on days when raining, used over short period of time till new 12volt battery could be sourced.

ROVER 200/25/ZR CENTRE ARMREST  

Featured here shown initially applied to the 200, then transferred into the MG at later date, a most sort after add on was the centre armrest, not really too good as storage area, but looks neat, and compensates filling in the void between front seats, a direct fit for the 200/25/ZR 

EXHAUST MANIFOLD HEAT SHIELD  

Shortened by several inches due to lower bolt hole corroding, they all do that, then constantly rattle. So on the lower cut edge its then folded over and hammered which also makes it more rigid, still gives a good coverage, then applied a coat of industrial strength alloy paint..

TRAVEL COMPANION DESIGNED FOR THE MG-ZR  

All wood detachable multi section construction, an ideal addition for any day away ventures, and will hold, with easy and quick access of tools, coolant, oil, spare bulbs, fuses, extinguisher etc along with most other on the go emergency items, that may be needed along the way.
