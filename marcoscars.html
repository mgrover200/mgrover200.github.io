1971 Marcos 3litre V6 in Chrome Yellow, Washed and polished ready for a early morning drive in convoy with other marcos owners down the M1 from Woolley Edge Services, to the 1977 International Marcos Rally held then at Castle Donington, other venues also included Weston Park.

1971 Marcos 3 Litre V.6 in Chrome Yellow 

Marcos 3 litre Ford V6. Special request from a family member, featuring ownership of their classic vehicle during the 1970's. 

The kit car built fibreglass body was attached to box frame chassis 3M/5849 powered by a Ford Zodiac V6 engine, first registered on March 1st 1971 with leicestershire registeration number of XNR 778J..parts and fittings sourced from numerous vehicles, would include Triumph, Vauxhall, MG, Austin, Porsche, Commer, and Hillman Hunter etc.  

Here we have a couple of randomly selected photo's, and most probably from well over 40 years ago. This venue was at the Turnpike Motel in Wetherby, just off the A1 on a really cold february morning. Preserved from this meeting are a good number of photos, including a short cine film footage. 

This local meeting, later in the year, and shown left, was at the Cross Keys in Skipton during May 1976. Although regarded as the year of the heatwave and subsequent drought. This event however was cold wet and windy during May of this photo session.. 

Marcos cars were always admired in the early years, from well known names to those who for a period of time actually owned one, Shown here are a just a random few from my own list which has been compiled over the years.

Rod Stewart (Singer) before upgrading to Lamborghinis. Paul Stassino (Actor) Alan Ball (Chelsea FC) Mick Robertson (Magpie TV Presenter) John Noakes (Blue Peter) Andy Fairweather Low lead singer of Amen Corner had one commisioned in Pink. Simon Park (of 'eye level' fame, the Van der Valk TV Series theme tune of 1972) 

Scott Engel and John Maus of The Walker Brothers fame both owned one each in red and blue, An old edition of..Record Mirror in our collection from February 1966...shows them looking out of the sunroof while kneeling on the transmission tunnel in each of their cars. A few others not necessarily owners...but possibly used the vehicles for films, racing, even photo shoots etc, were Jackie Stewart, John Lennon, Cliff Richard and including the Beatles. 

Roger Moore (Saint TV Series) As a racing driver, drove a white Marcos in an early February 1969 episode. 'The World Beater' this along with many more in my list of well known owners and admirers, of this classic car from years gone by.

As previous contributors of Marcos Owners Club, we have built up an extensive library of videos and photo's, of which includes a collection of some very rare cine film footage of early meetings, both local and national preserved from throughout 1970's, up to the early 1990's.
