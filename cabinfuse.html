CABIN FUSE PANEL 2004 ZR 

About features, and driver notifications on our MG-Rover Information Pages

Information page concentrates specifically on our 2004 MG ZR cabin located fuse box, of which circuit each fuse, and relays serves and protects, contents vary between different models and years, and so should only be regarded as a basic reference guide.

Fuse's and Relay's 2004 ZR

Relay 1 Window Lift Front. Relay 2 Window Lift Rear. Relay 3 Rear Wiper. Relay 4 Seat Heat. Relay 5 Window Lift Isolate. Relay 6 Alarm Sounder Relay 7 Sunroof. Relay 8 DLR

Fuses in the main panel are usually colour coded, and range from 5 amp to 30 amp The fuse panel will only accept the narrow pointed mini blade fuses shown here, along with the fuse puller. The blue flat blade fuse shown, may fit other fuse panels, but not our own 2004 ZR  

The cable and connector shown across front of the fuse panel, formed part of a project we were still working on a couple of winters ago, The fitting of driver and front passenger heated seat elements, a good choice for the MG and with a quick warm up time of only several seconds.

Information is referenced only to our 1996 Rover 200 and 2004 ZR 120+
