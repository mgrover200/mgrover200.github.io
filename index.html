YXB-100350 IGNITION SWITCH

To fit the new YXB-100350 ignition switch, steering wheel is lowered down column covers removed, top part will just prise off, bottom part secured with 3 small  screws, cabin fuse box lid also needs removing, switch is attached to bottom of the key barrel.

ENGINE OIL LEVEL DIPSTICK

The oil level dipsticks used on the 200 and ZR are not the best for showing a precise reading, plastic one for the 200, will in most cases eventually snap leaving tip of it floundering in the sump after many years of useage, wire one is not much better.

MORE INFO

INLET MANIFOLD GASKET

Always found the better option when replacing the inlet manifold gasket is not just detached from cylinder head while new gasket is fitted, but removed from vehicle, its not that much more time consuming, and makes it easier to work on.
